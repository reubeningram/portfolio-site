Black Panther

Interesting facts about the most awesomest animal:

  • Scientific Name: Panthera pardus
  • Average Length: 1.1m - 1.9m (43in - 75in)
  • Average Lifespan: 12 - 15 years
  • Habitat: Forest, swampland and grasslands

Black Panthers can reach a speed of 71 mph, there are 31 different species, and they prey on deer, tapir, and wild boar. Their predators are humans, lions and, the hyena.
